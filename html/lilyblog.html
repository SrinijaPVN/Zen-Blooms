Blossoming Care: A Guide to Preserving Your Flower Bouquet

Immediate Care:

Upon receiving the bouquet, unwrap it gently and trim the stems at an angle. Remove any excess foliage.

Water is Key:

Fill a clean vase with lukewarm water and flower food. Change the water every two days.

Placement Matters:

Keep the bouquet away from direct sunlight, heat sources, and drafty areas. Find a cool spot with good air circulation.

Regular Maintenance:

Check the water level daily, trim the stems, and remove any wilting flowers promptly.

Drying and Pressing:

To preserve beyond its natural lifespan, consider drying or pressing the flowers.

Enjoy the Beauty:

Take a moment each day to appreciate the beauty of your bouquet.
